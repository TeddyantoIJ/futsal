WHO WE ARE ?

We provide a platform for users to book futsal courts, invite matches, follow teams and much more online via mobile or web applications.


Mission

To provide the largest apps that enable sport field booking and social interaction among the users.

Vision

To increase people engagement, through doing group sport activities that promotes consistency , fairness, and competitiveness

Goal

To provide business tools for field owner that will enable them to optimize the occupancy rate of the field
